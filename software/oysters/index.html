Details and Implementation
Grant-funded research projects in collaboration with researchers at the University of Rhode Island and Rhode Island School of Design.
The Eastern Oyster species is being killed off by a bacterial pathogen, but one strain of the oyster is immune. URI researchers conducted experiments with succeptible and immune oysters exposed to the bacteria and measured their response over a period of time. The software tool visualizes a network representing gene similarity in the Eastern Oyster.
